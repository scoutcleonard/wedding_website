Visting the Bay Area

In our 6 years of life here, we have come to love the East Bay and the greater Bay Area. We’ll be overjoyed to have so many of our loved ones visiting for our wedding weekend. Whether you’re just looking for lodging recommendations, or planning to stay and explore beautiful California, you’ll find some of our favorite recommendations below.

This is just a start, but we could talk about Berkeley, Oakland, San Francisco, Marin, Yosemite, Tahoe, etc. etc. etc. at great length! Feel free to reach out if you want any specific recommendations; we’ll be so glad to help!

Where to Stay

The Gilman / Westbrae Neighborhood

This may not be a fair suggestion, as hotels are harder to come by, but this is our beloved neighborhood. We’ll plan to host in our cozy home for parts of the weekend, so if you can find an AirBnb in our walkable neighborhood, you’ll be close to the fun :)

Solano Ave. and North Berkeley BART are convenient from here.

For hotels, try:

  • The Berkeley Marina Double Tree
  • Aiden by Best Western
Downtown Berkeley

Downtown Berkeley is the city’s central location for restaurants and attractions. It’s near the beautiful UC Berkeley Campus and has access to the Downtown Berkeley BART Station - 1 stop from us. This neighborhood is also quite close to our ceremony venue, the UC Berkeley Botanical Garden!

You’ll find hotels to pick from around here, and some restaurants dear to us (Jupiter?!?)

For hotels, try:

  • The Hilton Graduate Hotel
  • Hotel Shattuck Plaza
  • Berkeley City Club
Emeryville

Emeryville is proximate to Berkeley and Oakland and is famously home to Pixar Studio’s and America’s Bike Mayor. It’s a bike- and predestrian-friendly city with access to Berkeley and Oakland. There are a few hotels with views of the Bay here!

For hotels, try:

  • Hyatt House Emeryville
  • Hyatt Place Emeryville
San Francisco

Though across the bay, San Francisco is just a BART ride away (~ 25-30 minutes from Downtown Berkeley) and could be fun if you’re coming from the East Coast! If you’re hoping to get some site-seeing in, this could be for you!

For hotels, try:

  • Hotel Zetta
  • The Marker Hotel

We just chose these two hotels for their BART access, but we’re certain SF has loads of more beautful, comfortable hotels. Let us know if we can help you find one!
